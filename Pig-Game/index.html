The Pig Game is a fun and competitive dice game where players take turns rolling a single die, aiming to accumulate points while avoiding the risk of losing it all. The goal is to be the first to reach a set score, typically 100, by strategically deciding whether to keep rolling or hold. Each roll adds to a player's round total, but rolling a 1 resets it to zero for that turn. With its mix of luck and strategy, the Pig Game is perfect for players of all ages seeking a blend of excitement and tension.
